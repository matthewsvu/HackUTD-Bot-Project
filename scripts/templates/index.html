The bots works with 4 functionalities or commands: $rmp $grades $grades $find $rmp fetches ratings from ratemyprofessors.com instantly. $grades displays a graph of student grades for a particular course and term, with the option of adding a specific professor. $find displays a particular professor's contact information. Format: : (ie. Spring 2020 is 20s, Fall 2019 is 19F) : (ie. cs1337, CHEM1311) and : Valid professor first and last names.
